INSTALL LUNAS TALAAN

Step 1: Open your phone's camera and scan the QR code above.

Step 2: Tap on the notification that appears to open the download link.

Step 3: Follow the instructions on the screen to install the application.

Step 4: Make sure to enable or allow “Install Unknown Apps” in your device setting.

Step 5: Once installed, open the Lunas Talaan app and sign up or log in.
